Who We Are

Rooted in Indian Hospitality

At All India Homestay, we believe travel is most meaningful when it feels personal. Founded in 2018, we started with a simple idea — to connect curious travelers with warm, welcoming Indian homes spread across 28 states.

"Every home we list is a living, breathing piece of India's incredible tapestry."

From the misty valleys of Himachal Pradesh to the backwaters of Kerala, from Rajasthan's desert dunes to Meghalaya's living root bridges — we curate stays that put you in the heart of local life, not just near a tourist attraction.

Our team personally visits and verifies every property, ensuring your comfort while preserving authenticity. We work directly with host families, supporting local livelihoods and sustainable tourism across rural and semi-urban India.

What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To create unforgettable, authentic experiences for every traveler through personalized service, genuine local culture, and a deep respect for the communities we operate in.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted homestay network — where every traveler feels at home, every host family thrives, and every corner of India gets to share its unique story with the world.

Our Values

Sustainability, community support, cultural respect, and transparency. We believe in responsible tourism that uplifts local economies and preserves the environment for future generations.
